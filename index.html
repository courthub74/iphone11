About.

From design to development, I am a web application maker. I develop applications to help budding coders gain valuable experience.


My brand is CourDevelops.


I am a user centric developer that realized my passion for the end user experience early in my tech career. Working tech support supervisor I once voluntarily made improvements to a tester that the backend engineers I worked with. That made all of our lives easier, and I was hooked on web development since.

I believe that the gig economy is the future of work. I want to contribute to it by developing apps that are easy to use and help budding web developers gain valuable work experience.
